About Us
Winners University is a growing, dynamic vision-driven University, founded on a Christian mission ethos and committed to pioneering excellence at the cutting edge of learning. We run with a compelling vision of raising a new generation of leaders for the African continent. We are doing this on a holistic, human development and integrated learning curriculum to raise total Men and Women who will develop their world.

Our Core-Values of Spirituality, Possibility Mentality, Capacity Building, Integrity, Responsibility, Diligence, and Sacrifice are what defines our commitment to excellence.

Why Choose Winners'?

Comprehensive Education
Degree programmes at Winners' University are all-encompassing. The University has developed custom-built courses to specially package the student and deliver him to his world upon graduation as an agent of change. He takes charge of his environment and breaks the barriers of limitation confronting our nation and continent at large.

Resources
The library is the heart and lifeline of any citadel of learning. The library complex is an edifice with three floors, reputed to be one of the largest in Africa. The total floor area of the complex is 11,300m2

Financial Aid
Winners' University offers international scholarships, fellowships, or grants to both local and international students. Apart from university-based scholarships, many other organizations, including foundations, trusts, and corporate bodies, give scholarships to international students.

Active Alumni Network
The University has Alumni network with more than 20,000 engaged members in more than 50 countries, exclusive alumni platform and job opportunities, more than 15% Alumni are Entrepreneurs (Job Creators) and Graduates excel as Ivy League Postgraduate students

Campus
Winners' campus is an ultra-modern new generation campus. Her luscious lawns, beautifully planned gardens and architectural masterpiece buildings provide a unique, stimulating and empowering context for inspiring research and creative activities.

Global Rankings
Winners' University has had a rapid rise in nearly two decades of its existence. The webometric ranking named Covenant University the best Private University in Nigeria in its 2021 ranking and the second best university overall.
